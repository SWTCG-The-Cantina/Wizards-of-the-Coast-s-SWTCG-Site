Hasbro Unleashed Action Figure Promo Cards


Unleashed Promo Cards have been exclusively packaged with the Hasbro Star Wars Unleashed Set 1 action figures and can be distinguished from other cards by the lightsaber logo appearing in the left corner of the card picture.
