Convention News - Gen Con 2003


Join Wizards of the Coast as we welcome Shannon McRandle (Mara Jade), Michonne Bourriague (Aurra Sing), Daniel Logan (Boba Fett), and Amy Allen (Aayla Secura) to Gen Con 2003 in Indianapolis.

These Star Wars fan favorites will be appearing in the Wizards booth and signing art based on their Star Wars Trading Card Game images.

This will also be your first opportunity to see Shannon's new Mara Jade image that will be used on the upcomming Mara Jade Star Wars Trading Card Game card, so be sure to come by and check it out!
