Gen Con So Cal
SW:TCG Lightsaber Tournament

This year at Gen Con So Cal, Wizards of the Coast will be holding a Star Wars TCG Lightsaber Tournament.

First place will receive a Master Replica Darth Vader Force FX Edition Lightsaber. Plus, Star Wars TCG booster packs will go to all top players.

Format for this event is Sanctioned Constructed (official rules may be read here), so bring your best Light Side and Dark Side decks.

The event will take place Saturday, December 4th at 10 AM in the TCG hall.
