Jedi Guardians Preview: Is my head on straight?


When C-3PO's head and body were seperated and ended up in the Geonosian Droid Factory's assembly line, 2 rather unique droids were created.

The Battle Protocol Droid is a great tool for card drawing, something the Dark Side needed more of. It might even stand up to an attack on occasion.

The Protocol Battle Droid has fairly good stats for its low build cost. However, it'll rarely get a chance to roll an attack, as the Light Side player will usually pay the 4 Force and deny it the chance to roll. Instead of relying on it for combat duty, think of it as a way to make the Light Side give up some precious Force points.
