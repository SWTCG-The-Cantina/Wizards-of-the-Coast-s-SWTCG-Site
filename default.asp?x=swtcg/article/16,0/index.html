Card Errata for ANH/BOY Recinded!
Greedo (A) & Greedo's Marksmanship - Re-Revised

Due to the fact that it was April 1st, we have recinded our decision to make Greedo shoot before Han Solo does. After watching the original VHS tapes of A New Hope, we have confirmed that indeed, Han Solo IS faster than Greedo. Thus we are changing back the cards to keep them as printed. We apologize for the confusion. It seems to always happen on April 1st.

Greedo (A)

  • Speed is officially 40 not 60.

    • Greedo's Marksmanship

  • Rules text has been kept at:
