You Make The Card!



With the new year comes exciting news for Star Wars TCG Players. Starting in 2003, you will the chance to help members of R&D design a card for an upcoming expansion! Over the next several weeks you will be given the chance to chime in with your vote in order to make decisions directly related to the creation of a card which will appear in a near future set.

Starting on January 15th visitors to the website who are logged in as members will have the chance to make the initial decision regarding what type of card we will make. So while we're keeping the details close to our vest until next week, you can start thinking of what type of card you would want to see in a future set.

Would you want to make a character? A ship? Maybe a cool Battle card! Would it be light, neutral or dark? What kind of new ability would you want this card to have or would you improve on an existing one?

All of these questions and much more will be asked of you as we allow You to Make the Card!

If you haven't had a chance to register to the wizards.com website before, you can prepare to be ready for next week's vote by Registering a Login ID.

Question:
On What Turn Does Your Typical Game End?

Results:

Behind the Scenes Poll
Over the Holiday Break we asked you to tell us how long your typical game lasts. Surprisingly, most of your games are going on for more than 11 turns, while we start to see a lot of games starting to end as soon as turn 3. This shows that while the majority of people find that their games are going into the "late game", it is possible to win early if you get out to a good start. One of our goals in R&D is to look at results like these and help us to determine what changes need to be made so that we can alter the game dynamics to appeal to what the players want. In the end, consistency and enjoyment are the two keys we are always striving for.

Look for You Make The Card starting next week!
