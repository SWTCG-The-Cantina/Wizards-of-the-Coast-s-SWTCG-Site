Phantom Menace Preview Card



Unseen enemies and unknown dangers lurk within the upcoming Phantom Menace expansion. To help introduce the set, we present the following preview card: Anakin's Podracer.

Anakin’s Podracer illustrates a new card mechanic from the Phantom Menace expansion: Lucky. Each attack, you may reroll a Lucky unit's attack dice or have your opponent reroll attack dice against it.
