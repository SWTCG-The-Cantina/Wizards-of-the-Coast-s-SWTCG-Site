Jedi Guardians Preview: Qui-Gon Jinn


Qui-Gon Jinn (B)

After much speculation as to when and where he would appear, Qui-Gon makes his debut in Jedi Guardians. Qui-Gon's special ability is very powerful, but it's hard to make it happen. If you have another Jedi in the Character arena, the Dark Side player will certainly attack it first, so that when Qui-Gon does go, there won't be anyone for him to untap. The key to making it work is to pair Qui-Gon with a stacked Jedi with a good Evade ability. That way the Dark Side will probably attack Qui-Gon first, just to reduce the number of attack dice you're rolling each turn.
