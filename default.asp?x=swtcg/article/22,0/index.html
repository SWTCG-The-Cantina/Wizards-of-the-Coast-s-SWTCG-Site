Jedi Guardians Preview: Ki-Adi-Mundi (A)
Last week we showed you the Overkill ability, but held the other new ability back; saved for future use or a special purpose.

Ki-Adi-Mundi (A)

Jedi Guardians

Rare

The other new ability in Jedi Guardians is called Reserves. Only a unit that is untapped and in the build zone can use its Reserves ability. Jedi Council member Ki-Adi-Mundi (A)'s Reserves ability reads:

Choose an arena. Remove 1 damage counter from each of your units in that arena. Play only when no unit is attacking (and only if Ki-Adi-Mundi is in your build zone).

While it isn't every day that you'll want to leave a Jedi out of the fightin', occasionally it's well worth it to have some Reserves.
