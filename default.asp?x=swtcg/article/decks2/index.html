Star Wars TCG - Playtest Cards

Cards before they were Cards!



Long before a card makes it to print, our Research and Development team creates proxy cards using printouts from their design database. These proxy cards are pasted onto available cardstock so that team members can play with the cards as they currently exist in the design environment.

During the design phase, the cards will go through minor changes in order to balance out the cards of the set. Below are a few playtest cards from A New Hope that had some minor changes right before the set was released. Click the Card Name to see the final versions.

Vader's Tie Fighter (A)

Final Version
Vader's Tie Fighter (B)
In the final version, this was changed to the (B) card and had the Shields 1 removed in lieu of an additional force activated Power 1 as well as an increased cost from 3 to 4. A new Vader's Tie Fighter (A) was then created with even more umph.
