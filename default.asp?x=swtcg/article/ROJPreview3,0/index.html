Return of the Jedi: Third Look
Zap!
David Cornford

Every Dark Side player has a Light Side Character that just really grates on them. For me, it’s Princess Leia (G). Once she gets on board the Falcon, I just have to sit back and watch my opponent build unit after unit with their extra build points. I can’t do anything about it.

Not any more.

X Force = X damage.

Pretty good, huh?

Not only that, but unless your opponent has a Jedi Master in play, that damage is unpreventable! Kiss that Leia! Adios Luke! Need an extra hit to take out that annoying stacked Obi-Wan Kenobi? Blam!

X Force. X dice of damage.

Any Character, any arena. Sure Yoda (E) can prevent that damage, but that's a slim lifeline to hold onto.

Prior to Return of the Jedi, high Force totals were just annoying values to track. Now they hold deadly threats. We’ll show you just how deadly in our next preview.
