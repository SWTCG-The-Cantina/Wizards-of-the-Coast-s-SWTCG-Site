Star Wars: Trading Card Game Card Sets


Wizards of the Coast releases new cards for the Star Wars Trading Card Game in sets called "expansions." Each expansion has its own unique list of cards that add new and exciting Characters, Ground units and Space units to the game. Below you will find a listing of these card set pages where you can find basic information about the set, card lists, and a variety of other useful facts.

The expansions are listed with the most recent release first. Click on an expansion name to go to that set's homepage.
