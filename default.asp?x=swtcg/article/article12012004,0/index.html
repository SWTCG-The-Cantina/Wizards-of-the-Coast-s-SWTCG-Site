December 2004 DCI Banned List Announcement—Star Wars TCG


Announcement Date: December 1, 2004
Effective Date: December 20, 2004

Constructed

  • Duel of the Fates is banned

Watch List
The following cards are on R&D's Watch List; they will be closely monitoring decks that use these cards:

  • Occupied Tatooine
  • Free Tatooine
  • Han's Promise
  • Blockade {TPM}
  • Chewbacca (G)
