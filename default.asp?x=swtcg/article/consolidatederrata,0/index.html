Star Wars TCG Consolidated FAQ,
Rulings, and Errata
The Big One

Every new Star Wars TCG set introduces cool new features to game. With each cool feature, though, comes a few questions. Over the last three years, some cards have received errata. To fully understand these features, answer these questions, and find this errata, one had to go to the proper expansion's page first. Not any more.

The Star Wars TCG Consolidated FAQ, Rulings, and Errata document is now available for download in PDF format. You need the most recent version of Adobe Reader (available free from Adobe) to view this file.

  • Star Wars TCG Consolidated FAQ, Rulings, and Errata (264k ZIP/PDF)

If you plan on participating in the Star Wars TCG 2005 Premier Series and Championship, don't leave home without checking this out first.
